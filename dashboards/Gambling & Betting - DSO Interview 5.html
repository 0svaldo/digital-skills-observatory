1/3 of the respondents bet - big differences between gender and group

About 1/3 of the respondents say they bet. For men this is a bit over half, and for women only 17%.

Most of the questions hereafter are for respondents who answered 'Yes' to 'Do you bet?'. Since we have only 13 female respondents for those questions, we won't compare them by gender.

There is also a noticeable difference between groups: 42% of the treatment group bets, while only 30% of the control group does.
